- **Cybersecurity practices** (e.g., password management, software updates).  
- **Protection of personal information** (financial, social, and sensitive data).  
- **Internet-connected devices** (computers, smartphones, IoT devices).  
- **Routine measures** to reduce exposure to threats.  

This concept is distinct from isolated solutions like antivirus software and does not guarantee complete risk elimination, but it forms a critical foundation for digital safety.
