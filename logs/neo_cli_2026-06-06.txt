Individuals who present good cyber hygiene follow security best practices and are more likely to protect their personal information effectively. Addressing noncompliance with these practices is a relevant concern in designing effective strategies. By combining technical controls with attention to how people use them, we can better reduce exposure to cyber threats.

*   Adopt routine security habits when using internet-connected devices
*   Protect personal, financial, and social information during online activity
*   Follow security guidelines that reduce exposure to cyber threats
*   Maintain secure behavior across devices, accounts, networks, and online communication
*   Encourage individuals to adopt good cyber hygiene practices
